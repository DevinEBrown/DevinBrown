About Google Classroom

Google Classroom is an inclusive tool for teaching and learning. Educators use Google Classroom to organize, measure, and enrich learning activities.

The Integrated Experience

As a middle school teacher who recently transitioned to online learning due to the pandemic, I recognized the need to provide teachers with credible Google Classroom tools to boost their efficiency.

Scenario #2

Mrs. Minks follows the current district policy on taking attendance, stating that online students must turn their cameras on during attendance. After taking attendance, students may turn their cameras back off. Mrs. Minks spends 20 minutes of a 55 minute class period taking attendance to ensure all students are present. After taking attendance, Mrs. Minks now has 35 minutes of instruction time. She feels this is not enough time to teach her students the required standards effectively.
