About

Hey there! I'm Devin, a passionate Web & UX Designer and Researcher. With a knack for blending creativity with functionality, I specialize in crafting intuitive digital experiences that captivate users. Over the past year, I've successfully launched several projects tailored to address specific user needs and preferences. My academic background has enriched my journey, holding a Master's in Human-Computer Interaction, an Associate's in Computer Programming, and a Bachelor's in Business Administration.

My expertise lies in unraveling the complexities of user interactions and translating them into seamless design solutions. Whether conducting user research, wireframing, or prototyping, I thrive on the challenges of creating delightful user experiences.

Previously, I was a computer and digital technology teacher, where I taught courses in web development, robotics, digital media, and I led an award-winning yearbook staff.

As I look to the next phase of my career, I'm eager to dive deeper into projects that push the boundaries of web design and user experience. I'm driven by a desire to innovate and solve real-world problems through thoughtful design.

I enjoy traveling, photography, and trying out new recipes in the kitchen in my free time.
